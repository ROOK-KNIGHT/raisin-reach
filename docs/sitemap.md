# RaisinRach Sitemap & Architecture

## 1. Core Navigation Structure

### 1.1 Home (`/`)
*   **Hero Section:** "20 Years. Zero Excuses. Your Calendar, Full."
    *   *Visual:* High-impact typography, subtle gold accents.
    *   *CTA:* "Calculate Your ROI" (Anchor to Calculator) / "Book Strategy"
*   **The "Method" (Scroll Animation):**
    *   *Content:* Step-by-step visualization of the cold calling process (Research -> Connection -> Value -> Close).
    *   *Interaction:* Liquid motion reveals steps as user scrolls.
*   **Results/Proof (Case Study Grid):**
    *   *Content:* Quantitative results (e.g., "$2M Pipeline Generated", "300% Appointment Increase").
    *   *Format:* Brutalist grid with monospace data points.
*   **The "Rach" Factor (Personal Brand):**
    *   *Content:* Rach's 20-year pedigree, philosophy ("Anti-Script"), and human approach.
    *   *Visual:* Professional, high-trust portrait.
*   **Appointment ROI Calculator (Lead Magnet):**
    *   *Function:* Interactive tool to estimate potential revenue from professional appointment setting.
*   **Footer:**
    *   Links to Legal, Socials, Knowledge Graph entry points.

### 1.2 "The Method" (Deep Dive) (`/method`)
*   Detailed breakdown of the proprietary cold calling techniques.
*   FAQs (structured data for "People Also Ask").

### 1.3 Case Studies (`/results`)
*   Individual pages for major wins (e.g., `/results/tech-saas-scale`).
*   Focus on "Problem -> RaisinRach Intervention -> Revenue Outcome".

### 1.4 About Rach (`/about`)
*   Extended bio, philosophy, and media appearances.

### 1.5 Contact (`/contact`)
*   Direct calendar booking (Calendly/Cal.com embed).
*   Inquiry form for larger enterprise contracts.

## 2. SEO & AEO Strategy: "Cold Calling Knowledge Graph" (`/knowledge`)

This section is designed to build Topical Authority and target long-tail queries.

### 2.1 Category Structure
*   `/knowledge/strategies` (e.g., "Gatekeeper Navigation", "Objection Handling")
*   `/knowledge/industries` (e.g., "Cold Calling for SaaS", "Cold Calling for Real Estate")
*   `/knowledge/leadership` (e.g., "Scaling Sales Teams", "Outsourcing vs. In-house")

### 2.2 Content Architecture
*   **Article Format:**
    *   `H1`: Clear, keyword-rich title.
    *   `Summary`: AI-friendly summary (2-3 sentences) at the top.
    *   `Key Takeaways`: Bullet points for quick scanning.
    *   `Schema`: `Article`, `FAQPage`, `HowTo`.
    *   `Internal Linking`: Tight interlinking between "Strategies" and "Industries".
